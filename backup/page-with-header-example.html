About US 

We would like to introduce ourselves as proud co-owners of newly opened luxurious hotel called JP heights, having 10 spacious well crafted bedrooms, with an exquisite boardroom & a multi-cuisine rooftop restaurant managed by Ingesta international that serves Indian, Chinese and Continental dishes. With its friendly and relaxed atmosphere, this hotel offers all the peace and comfort of a private home where people can find exclusively, quality and friendly service.

JP Heights is located in one of the posh areas of Rourkela, adjacent to Hotel Mayfair and next to Hotel Shalimar. It is just 2 minutes walk from the Reliance City Centre & PSR Multiplex. 10 Mins ride from Airstrip & Railway Station. In a city full of life, JP Heights is an oasis of relaxation and tranquility.
