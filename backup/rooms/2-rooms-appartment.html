Ingesta International is STYLISH, ELEGANT AND MODERN that serves Indian, Chinese and Continental dishes. With its friendly and relaxed atmosphere, this hotel offers all the peace and comfort of a private home where people can find exclusively, quality and friendly service.

Il This Boutique Hotel is elegantly styled, fully serviced suites, offer a world of luxury, comfort and warmth, matched with impeccable service and a range of ecletic cuisines, to delight the discecerning business traveler.

Il Ingesta serves breakfast from 8 to 11 am, Lunch 12 to 4 PM and dinner from 5 pm onwards. Seating is available indoors and on the rooftop. Perhaps the area’s most coveted and unconventional space, features a lush vertical scenic beauty  and striking skylight, casting away any thoughts of Rourkela’s concrete jungle.
