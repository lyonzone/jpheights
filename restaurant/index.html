Restaurant

RESTAURANT JP HEIGHTS  is STYLISH, ELEGANT AND MODERN that serves Indian, Chinese and Continental dishes. With its friendly and relaxed atmosphere, this hotel offers all the peace and comfort of a private home where people can find exclusively, quality and friendly service.
This Boutique Hotel is elegantly styled, fully serviced suites, offer a world of luxury, comfort and warmth, matched with impeccable service and a range of ecletic cuisines, to delight the discecerning business traveler.
RESTAURANT JP HEIGHTS serves breakfast from 8 to 11 am, Lunch 12 to 4 PM and dinner from 5 pm onwards. Seating is available indoors and on the rooftop. Perhaps the area’s most coveted and unconventional space, features a lush vertical scenic beauty and striking skylight, casting away any thoughts of Rourkela’s concrete jungle.
