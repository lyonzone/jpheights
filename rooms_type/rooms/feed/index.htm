Overview

Exuding the originality and out-of-the-box creativity that makes Rourkela and by extension, JP Heights features state-of-the-art space for small meetings for up to 25 guests. The go-to gathering spot for the neighborhood’s premier advertising, design and communication firms, Corporates, the conference hall offers expert meeting and special event planning, including a Home Theater and catering by Ingesta.

For social events with breathtaking views of the Rourkela, there is no better venue than JP Height’s with its mesmerizing rooftop restaurant and lounge. Available for private events for up to 30 to 40 guests, this high-rise retreat is poised to take its place among the city’s favorite meeting place.
